The Place Inventory lists the distinct places on the property.
Distinct places can overlap, or one place can be fully contained
within another.  Every part of the property should be at least one
place, however.

Place is one of three attributes associated with each requirement:
place, skill, and feeling.

 - House
    - kitchen
    - bedroom
    - bathroom
 - Patio
 - Straw Bale Studio
    - Covered Porch
 - Shed

 - Driveway
